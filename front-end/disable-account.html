Disable Your Account

Are you sure you want to disable your account?

Disabling your account will cause the following:

  • All pending withdrawals will be canceled.
  • All tokens from your account will be disabled.
  • All API for your account will be deleted.
  • Tokens will be returned to Zantepay foundation without refunding. After tokens are generated and distributed to users and account is reactivated after the ICO, tokens will be lost forever.

You will need to contact support@zantepay.com when you decide to reactivate your account.
